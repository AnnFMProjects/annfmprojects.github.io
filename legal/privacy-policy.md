# Privacy Policy — FM Home Loans Internal Tools

*Last updated: 2026-07-21*

## What this covers

This describes how the internal tools at this site — the Closing Cost Estimator (CCE), Pre-Approval Generator (PAG), Social Media Tool, Greatness Tracker, and the Admin App (collectively, the "Tools") — handle information. These Tools are for internal use by FM Home Loans loan officers and staff, not the general public.

**This Privacy Policy sits on top of, and does not replace, FM Home Loans' own Privacy Policy** ([fmm.com/privacy](https://www.fmm.com/privacy)), which governs FM Home Loans' relationship with its customers and the public more broadly. Where anything here is silent, that policy still applies.

## Information collected

Depending on which Tool is used, this may include:

- **Account information** for staff logging in — name, email, and password (stored as a salted hash, never in plain text).
- **Client/loan information** entered by staff while using a Tool — for example, a borrower's name, loan amounts, property value, loan type, credit score band, and processing/checklist status. These Tools do not ask for or store Social Security numbers, account/routing numbers, tax ID numbers, or full credit reports.
- **Basic usage information** needed to run the Tool (e.g., a session token stored in your browser to keep you logged in).

## How information is used

Information entered is used solely to provide the Tool's functionality to permitted staff — generating estimates, saved-client records, exports, and similar internal outputs. It is not sold, and is not shared outside FM Home Loans and its service providers needed to run these Tools (e.g., the backend infrastructure they run on).

## Storage and retention

Information entered into these Tools may be retained indefinitely, or for as long as applicable law permits and legitimate business needs require, consistent with FM Home Loans' broader recordkeeping practices. There is currently no automatic deletion schedule for saved client/loan records.

## Access

Access to information stored by these Tools is limited to FM Home Loans personnel who have been granted permission by Ann Zeilingold (or her designee), and only for the specific tool/permission they've been granted. Permissions can be changed or revoked at any time.

## Security

These Tools use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards appropriate for an internal business tool — including account authentication, permission-gated access, and password hashing. No system is perfectly secure, and these Tools are still in development.

## Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time as the Tools develop. Continued use after an update means you accept the current version.

## Questions

Direct any questions about this Privacy Policy to Ann Zeilingold.
